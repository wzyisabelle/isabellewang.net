Vernal Soirée

Vernal Soirée is a multi-sensory experience that reimagines the literati wine-drinking gatherings of China's Song Dynasty as a game for contemporary audiences. A set of hand-drawn cards structures a sequence of ritualized interactions, while digital projections and 3D-modeled scenography extend the tabletop into an immersive spatial experience. Players are invited into a room where the choreography of drinking, reciting, and playing carries the sociality of a thousand-year-old tradition into the present.

2023. Immersive experience. Card game design, ritual design, digital projection, 3D scenography.
