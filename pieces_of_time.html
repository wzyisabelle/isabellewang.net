Pieces of Time

Under the division of time zones, what does our life look like in each other's eyes?

This browser-based work maps the day into four colored blocks representing sleep, work, personal time, and preferred hours for connection. By entering their own schedule and time zone, users see their daily rhythm rendered as a moving composition, and can view someone else's life unfolding in parallel on the other side of the world. The link was shared with friends across continents, whose responses came back as unique visual traces, weaving a soft connection through fragmented hours.

2024. Interactive web installation. p5.js, real-time data.

Audience interactions at the pop-up exhibition, Goldsmiths, January 2025.
